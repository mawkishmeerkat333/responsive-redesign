Benvenuto!

Welcome to our restaurant. We specialize in Italian cuisine. Our recipes stem from our roots in Napoli, Italy. Here at Amalfi's we use only the finest ingredients and the freshest products available. Pizza, pasta, strombolis, calzones, subs, traditional Italian entrees and more! From our delicious homemade sauces to our wonderful homemade dough and breads, you find them all superb. We hope to see you soon. A presto! Grazie!
